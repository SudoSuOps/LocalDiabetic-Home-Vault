# Hospital Records

**Discharge papers, hospital summaries, and after-visit instructions go here.**

When you come home from the hospital or a visit, drop the paperwork in this folder —
scans, photos, or PDFs. Keeping it in one place means the next doctor, nurse, or
family member can find it fast.

## Helpful to capture

- Discharge date: ____________________
- Reason for visit: ____________________
- Follow-up instructions: ____________________
- New medications started: ____________________ (also update `../01-medications/`)
- Next follow-up appointment: ____________________ (also add to `../10-appointments/`)

*These records stay on your box. They never sync to the cloud automatically.*
