# Insurance & Benefits

**Put insurance cards, benefits letters, and coverage documents here.**

Photos or scans of cards, approval/denial letters, Explanation of Benefits (EOB),
and pharmacy benefit info all live in this folder. They stay **here, on your box** —
they never leave the vault automatically (see `../HARD-INVARIANT.md`).

give legal or medical advice.*
