# Care-Pack Request

**When life gets hard, ask for help here.** A care-pack is a request that a family
member, local volunteer, vendor, or sponsor can fulfill.

> Filling this out does not send anything anywhere automatically. It organizes the
> ask so a helper can act on it. Nothing leaves your vault without your say-so.
