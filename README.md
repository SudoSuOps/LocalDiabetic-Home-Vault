# LocalDiabetic Home Vault

**A private home folder system for diabetic records, reminders, supplies, appointments, and family support.**

*Powered by OpenDiabetic.*

> **📖 Read [WHY.md](WHY.md) first** — the founding story, in the founder's words. It's
> why this exists, and it's the spec for every decision here.

---

## What this is

This is **your vault**. It lives on your own computer or home NAS box. It holds the
papers, lists, and reminders that keep diabetic life organized — all in one place,
in plain folders you can open yourself.

When someone comes home from the hospital, everything is scattered: discharge papers,
new medications, supply boxes, appointment cards, insurance letters. This vault gives
it all a home.

**No app required to start.** These are just folders and simple documents. Open them
on any computer. Print them. Hand them to a family member. They work offline, forever.

## What this is **not**

- It is **not** a medical device.
- It does **not** diagnose, change medications, or replace your doctor.
- It does **not** send your records to the cloud. (See `HARD-INVARIANT.md`.)

This vault **organizes and reminds**. For any medical decision, talk to your clinician.

## The folders

| Folder | What goes here |
|---|---|
| `00-emergency-card` | One page a paramedic or stranger needs in an emergency. Fill this first. |
| `01-medications` | Every medication, dose, and schedule. |
| `02-doctors-pharmacy` | Phone numbers for doctors, pharmacy, care team. |
| `03-insurance` | Insurance cards, benefits letters, member IDs. |
| `04-hospital-records` | Discharge papers, hospital summaries, instructions. |
| `05-labs` | Lab results — A1C, glucose logs, bloodwork. |
| `06-foot-care` | Daily foot-check routine and log. Critical for diabetics. |
| `07-wound-care` | Wound and dressing-change tracking. |
| `08-supplies` | What you have, what's running low, what to reorder. |
| `09-food-cookbooks` | Food plans, shopping lists, diabetic-friendly recipes. |
| `10-appointments` | Upcoming visits + questions to bring to the doctor. |
| `11-family-helpers` | Who helps, with what, and how to reach them. |
| `12-care-packs` | Requests for foot kits, wound supplies, food boxes, rides. |
| `13-organized-notes` | Receipt-backed organization of your own papers. **Not medical advice.** |
| `14-receipts` | A log of every automated action taken on your behalf. Proof, not promises. |

---

## How to start (first 15 minutes)

1. Open `00-emergency-card/` and fill in the emergency card. This is the most important page.
2. Open `01-medications/` and list your current medications.
3. Open `02-doctors-pharmacy/` and add your doctor and pharmacy numbers.

That's it. Everything else you can fill in over time, a folder at a time.

## For family and helpers

If you are helping someone with this vault: start with the README in each folder.

The goal is simple — **when life gets hard, the information is already in order.**
