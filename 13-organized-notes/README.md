# Organized Notes

**Receipt-backed organization of your own papers — not medical advice.**

---

## What this folder is

When the system helps tidy your vault, the result lands here: a cleaned-up list, a
plain-language restatement of *your own* notes, a shopping list built from your food
plan, a set of questions pulled together for your next appointment.

Every item here is **organization**, not interpretation:

- ✅ "Here are your 4 medications, grouped by morning/evening, from your own list."
- ✅ "Here's a shopping list built from the food plan you saved."
- ✅ "Here are the questions you jotted down, gathered for Thursday's visit."
- ❌ It will **not** tell you what a lab result means.
- ❌ It will **not** suggest a medication or dose change.
- ❌ It will **not** diagnose or triage.

For anything medical, the note will say: **confirm with your clinician.**

## Why we renamed this

This folder used to be called "ai-summaries." We changed it on purpose.
"Summaries" invites the wrong expectation — that a machine read your medicine and
told you what to do. That is not what happens here. This is **receipt-backed
organization of papers you already have**, and every action leaves a receipt in
`14-receipts/`.

---

*Every note generated here points to a matching receipt. No receipt, no trust.*
